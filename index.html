.SPARK|

At the first iteration of SPARK, come together for 15 hours to turn their ideas into reality to make a possitive impact in our community.


Come to our maker-space, form teams with other participants, and work to build a product demo for judging at the end of the day. Throughout the event, you can participate in games and activities to get to know others and also connect with our corporate sponsors. We provide the food, you bring the creativity.


You’ll enjoy a weekend of awesome tech, food, swag, workshops, and mini-events while learning from distinguished speakers, inspiring mentors, and peers. We consider SPARK a success if you make new connections and gain skills that will stay with you long after SPARK is over.


Whether you’re a seasoned developer or designer, or just starting out as a beginner, there’s something at SPARK for you.

FAQ

  • What is a hackathon?

    It’s a grand gathering of people to make their ideas come to life. Think out of the box, try new technologies, ask questions, make mistakes, discover a new language, and have fun!</p><p>Check out a video explaining what a hackathon is here.

  • What if I have no experience with coding or hackathons?

    Don’t sweat it! We’ve purposefully designed SPARK to offer an encouraging and supportive environment. At SPARK, you’ll quickly learn from our workshops and army of mentors, as well as your team members and other students.

  • Who can attend?

    We welcome any college student 18+ to participate in SPARK. If you have any questions about this, please contact us!

  • How will mentorship work?

    We believe that mentorship is essential to an effective learning experience. You will have access to wonderful mentors throughout the entire event who will help guide you and help you troubleshoot, including industry professionals, ECU alums, faculty, and experienced SPARKERS.

  • How will teams work?

    Each team may have between 2-4 people. You and your friend may apply as a team, but everyone must submit individual applications. Members of teams who apply together will all receive the same acceptance decision.

    We’ll also be facilitating team-formation activities before and during the event to help you find teams. Although you’re welcome to hack on your own, only teams are eligible to win prizes.

  • What will I eat?

    Trust us, you won’t be hungry at SPARK! We’ll provide breakfast, lunch & dinner. We’ll also have a snack table, food surprises, and coffee throughout SPARK. We work hard to accommodate all dietary restrictions and make healthy options available.

  • What should I bring to SPARK?

    Please bring your student ID, laptop, chargers and any tools that you may need to get you SPARKING.

  • What if I can't stay the whole day?

    If you have a special circumstance, please mention it on your application. SPARK is ultimately a resource for you to learn, connect with others, and meet mentors in industry, so you'll get more out of it if you can stay for the whole event -but you don't have to :) .

  • Who do I contact with other questions?

    Shoot us a message at spark.ecu@gmail.com and we’ll get right back to you!
